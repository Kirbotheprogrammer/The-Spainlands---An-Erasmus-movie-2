For those who don’t know, there are four versions of An Erasmus Movie 2: the first, deleted due to the GREAT RESET While managing files for GMTK 2025, I accidentally deleted about 75% of my work. Luckily, my backup saved the rest, and starting from scratch, I managed to create something even better. the second, released on September 24; the third, an incomplete version; and finally, the fourth, the one you’re seeing now.

This fourth edition underwent a complete rewrite, with a revamped introduction (2 full minutes of new clips!) inspired by the famous fan opening of Part 9 of JoJo’s Bizarre Adventures JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure is a long-running manga series about the Joestar family and their adventures, often involving supernatural powers called Stands. The ninth part follows Jodio Joestar, a clever and determined young hero, who faces dangerous enemies and mysterious challenges. His Stand, November Rain, gives him unique abilities that make his journey even more intense and unpredictable. a manga I follow passionately, and whose latest volume I’ve just preordered. The End Poem was also revisited.

But what I truly love about this version are the two small clips recorded at Andrea’s house last week: we had so much fun! Perhaps it’s true… I’m never fully satisfied, but in a positive way.
